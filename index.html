My Story


As a rising college junior majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Data Science, I found inspiration in the profound impact technology has on our daily lives. It was the perfect blend of problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and engaging in collaborative projects that captivated my interest. The boundless creativity and innovation within the field, coupled with its potential to create meaningful change, have firmly solidified my passion for computer science as a platform for personal growth and making a lasting contribution to the future.
